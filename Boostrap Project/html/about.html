About Our Company

Founded in 1988, Cushions Ltd has been dedicated to crafting high-quality, stylish, and comfortable cushions for homes, offices, and outdoor spaces. With a strong commitment to innovation and design, we have grown from a small local business to a recognized name in the industry. Our journey began with a simple goal: to provide premium cushions that blend aesthetics with functionality, ensuring comfort and elegance in every setting

CEO & Our Story

At the heart of Cushions Ltd is our visionary CEO, Mr CEO, whose passion for comfort and design inspired the foundation of our company. Starting with a small workshop, the company quickly gained a loyal customer base thanks to its commitment to craftsmanship and quality. Over the years, Mr CEO has led the company through innovation and expansion, ensuring that every cushion we produce meets the highest standards

Our Products & Services

Cusions Ltd offers a wide range of cushions, including decorative pillows, ergonomic seat cushions, outdoor cushions, and custom-designed pieces tailored to individual preferences.
